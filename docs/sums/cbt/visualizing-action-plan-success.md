## Visualizing Action Plan Success

Clients are more likely to complete Action Plans when they visualize a positive outcome. Guide them to imagine a specific time in the coming week when they've just finished the task.

**The visualization:** Ask: "Can you imagine it's [day/time] and you've just finished [the task]? What do you see? How are you feeling?" Walk through the concrete details—where are they, what did they do, what comes next?

**Building in self-credit:** Guide them to imagine giving themselves credit in one of several forms:
- "It's good I did that"
- "I deserve credit for doing this"
- "That's good"
- "This is an important step for me"
- "This is going to help me reach my goal"

**Emotional depth:** Help them visualize not just the behavior but the positive emotion attached. Ask: "What's good about the experience? What does it mean to you? What does doing this say about you?" Let them experience some of that positive emotion right there in session with you.

This dual rehearsal—behavioral and emotional—creates a mental template they can draw on when motivation lags at home.

## The 3-Minute Breathing Space: Meditation in Daily Life

One challenge of MBCT is helping clients move from practicing in a quiet room to maintaining awareness during the chaos of daily life. The **3-Minute Breathing Space** bridges this gap by bringing a condensed version of sitting meditation into moments when the client most needs it.
